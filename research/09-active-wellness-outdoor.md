## 48. Religious festivals & processions

- **Festas de Santo António / Santos Populares** — citywide (June) — Lisbon's patron-saint celebrations: arraiais, Marchas Populares (12 June), Casamentos de Santo António mass weddings, and the Santo António procession. https://santosdelisboa.pt — *seasonal, June*
- **Procissão de Santo António** — Sé / Igreja de Santo António (Alfama) — Religious procession honouring the saint, around 13 June. — *seasonal*
- **Procissão de Nossa Senhora da Saúde** — Mouraria / Martim Moniz / Av. Almirante Reis — Historic procession (since 1570) on the 1st/2nd Sunday of May; tens of thousands attend; paired with the Procissão das Velas in the Baixa. https://getlisbon.com/discovering/procession-our-lady-health-lisbon/ — *seasonal, May*
- **Procissão do Senhor dos Passos da Graça** — São Roque → Chiado/Baixa → Graça — ~Lenten procession (since 1587), 1st Sunday of Lent (e.g. 1 Mar 2026, departs São Roque 14:30); seven stations linking São Roque to Igreja da Graça via Rossio and Largo São Domingos. https://scml.pt — *seasonal, Lent*
- **Procissão das Velas** — Baixa — Candlelit procession held alongside the Senhora da Saúde festivities in Mouraria/Baixa. — *seasonal, May*
- **Festa de São Vicente** — São Vicente de Fora (Alfama/Graça) — Feast of Lisbon's co-patron (late January); church/parish celebrations, tied to the city's crow symbolism. — *seasonal, January*
- **Romarias & saints' processions (greater Lisbon)** — Loures, Sesimbra (Senhor Jesus das Chagas), Sintra, Mafra (Círio) — Regional romarias with processions, bands and feasting through the year. — *seasonal*
- *Discovery note:* Patriarcado de Lisboa (patriarcado-lisboa.pt), Santa Casa da Misericórdia (scml.pt) and Agência Ecclesia publish exact procession dates each year; AgendaLX/Lisboa Cultura lists the civic side of Santo António.
