## 13. Markets & fairs

- **Feira da Ladra** — Alfama / Campo de Santa Clara. Lisbon's historic flea market; second-hand goods, antiques, vinyl, vintage clothes, crafts. Tuesdays & Saturdays ~09:00–18:00 (quieter on Tuesdays). https://informacao.lisboa.pt/en/schedule/what-to-do/flea-market
- **Mercado de Santa Clara** — Alfama / Campo de Santa Clara. Covered market building beside Feira da Ladra; antiques, books, crafts, a culinary-arts centre and the Casa da BD stall; same Tue/Sat rhythm. https://informacoeseservicos.lisboa.pt/contactos/diretorio-da-cidade/mercado-de-santa-clara
- **LX Market** — Alcântara / LX Factory. Sunday street market (~10:00–18:00) with makers, illustrators, designers, second-hand clothes and local-product stalls. Instagram @lxmarket / https://www.facebook.com/mercadocraftsdesign/
- **Time Out Market Lisboa** — Cais do Sodré / Mercado da Ribeira. Food hall programming concerts, DJ sets, theatre, Sing Out Bingo (musical bingo), comedy and fairs at the Time Out Studio. https://www.timeoutmarket.com/lisboa/eventos-e-espectaculos/ / Instagram @timeoutmarket
- **Mercado de Campo de Ourique** — Campo de Ourique. Gourmet food market with rotating art exhibitions, chess tournaments and occasional free concerts. Instagram @mercadodecampodeourique
- **Mercado Biológico do Príncipe Real (AgroBio)** — Príncipe Real. Weekly certified-organic farmers' market in the garden, Saturdays ~09:00–13:00; produce, breads, cheeses, wines. https://www.visitlisboa.com/en/places/principe-real-organic-produce-fair
- **Mercado Biológico AgroBio (Campo Pequeno)** — Avenidas Novas / Campo Pequeno. Certified-organic producers' market in the Campo Pequeno garden, Saturdays. https://www.visitlisboa.com/en/events/agrobio-organic-market
- **Mercado de Arroios** — Arroios / Largo do Intendente.
