# Batch 02 — Nightlife, parties, rooftops & hostels

Coverage for categories 3 (Nightlife & parties), 27 (LGBTQ+ scene & events), 34 (Rooftops & terraces with programming), and 35 (Hostels with public events & parties). Current as of June 2026. Seasonal items and possible closures are flagged inline.

## 3. Nightlife & parties

### Cais do Sodré / Pink Street (Rua Nova do Carvalho) & Cais do Gás
- **Lux Frágil** — Santa Apolónia (riverside, Av. Infante D. Henrique, Armazém A) — Lisbon's flagship club since 1998; two electronic dancefloors (techno/house/disco) plus rooftop terrace; residents Rui Vargas, Yen Sung, Tiago Miranda, DJ Dexter; added gender-neutral bathrooms and the bi-monthly queer EXOTIKA residency (2025). luxfragil.com — IG: @luxfragil — RA: ra.co/clubs/2369
- **B.Leza** — Cais do Sodré (Cais do Gás, riverside) — The city's Luso-African institution; kizomba, semba, funaná, Afro-house; live orchestras Thu/Fri, dance classes Sat, legendary Sunday kizomba sessions (classes ~8pm). IG: @blezaclube
- **Titanic Sur Mer** — Cais do Sodré (Cais do Gás, under the arches) — Jazz, samba, electronic, cabaret, live concerts and DJ sets in a vaulted riverside space; recurring "Roda de Samba do Titanic" (IG: @rodadesambadotitanic). IG: @titanicsurmer (concerts: @concertos.titanic)
- **Jamaica** — Cais do Sodré (relocated 2021 from R. Nova do Carvalho to Cais do Gás) — Decades-old institution; pop-rock classics, reggae, retro; mixed late crowd. IG: @discotecajamaica
- **Tokyo** — Cais do Sodré (relocated 2021 to Cais do Gás) — Live concerts plus karaoke, indie rock, disco, funk, R&B; live + DJ nights.
- **Europa** — Cais do Sodré (relocated 2021 to Cais do Gás) — Late-late electronic/eclectic club, fills 3am–dawn.
- **Pensão Amor** — Cais do Sodré (R. Nova do Carvalho 38) — Former brothel turned burlesque/cabaret bar; erotic-art rooms, back-room dancefloor, varied DJ styles, free entry. IG: @pensaoamor
- **Sol e Pesca** — Cais do Sodré (R. Nova do Carvalho 44) — Tinned-fish "conserveira" bar with regular live music and DJ nights, quintessential Pink Street spot. IG: @solepesca
- **Povo** — Cais do Sodré (R. Nova do Carvalho 32–36) — Restaurant/bar running year-round residencies for young fado singers ("fado vadio"), free, Tue–Sun ~8:30–11pm, then DJ/bar. IG: @povolisboa
- **O Bom O Mau e O Vilão** — Cais do Sodré (R. do Alecrim 21) — Bohemian multi-room bar with frequent live jazz/soul and DJ sets. IG: @obomomaueovilao
- **Lust in Rio** — Cais do Sodré (Cais do Gás, riverside) — Riverside club; Brazilian funk, house, R&B, pop, theme parties. IG: @lustinrio
- **Roterdão Club** — Cais do Sodré (R. Nova do Carvalho 7) — Indie, post-punk, electronic, plus live acts and theatre performances.
- **Plateau** — Cais do Sodré/Santos border (Escadinhas da Praia 7) — Long-running club, 80s/90s, rock, pop; ladies' nights; open to 6am.
- **Rive Rouge** — Cais do Sodré (atop Time Out Market / Mercado da Ribeira) — Cocktails and dancing, house/techno and curated music, afternoon-to-night. IG: @riverougelisboa
- **Musicbox** — Cais do Sodré (R. Nova do Carvalho 24) — **[CLOSED]** — Iconic 19-year live-music/club venue closed 15 Sept 2025 (neighbourhood "descaracterização" + rent); team and programming moved to Casa Capitão in Beato. musicboxlisboa.com — IG: @musicboxlisboa
- **Ministerium Club** — Terreiro do Paço / Praça do Comércio 72 (edge of Cais do Sodré/Baixa) — Techno and house, big-name and underground bookings; runs Ministerium Records (vinyl label, est. 2017); hosts Konda Records parties. IG: @ministeriumclub
- **Javá Rooftop** — Cais do Sodré (8 Building, 5th floor) — Tropical-themed rooftop bar with DJ music (also listed under Rooftops). IG: @javarooftop
