# Batch 04 — Cinema, film festivals, immersive & photography

Scope note: open-air cinema is heavily seasonal (roughly May–October); most operators publish full line-ups only in May/June, so dates below reflect the 2026 season where confirmed and the recurring annual pattern otherwise. Film-festival dates are 2026 editions where found.

## 6. Outdoor & Open-Air Cinema

- **Cine Society** — Príncipe Real / Carmo / riverside (rotating). Lisbon's largest open-air cinema operator; cult classics, Oscar winners and date-night picks on rooftops and docks. 2026 venues: Carmo Rooftop, Doca da Marinha (Tejo riverside) and a new Príncipe Real Terrace (terrace with castle view, sessions June–July only); season runs to mid-October, sessions ~21:00, tickets ~€14.50. https://cinesociety.pt — Instagram @cinesociety.lisbon
- **Black Cat Cinema** — Graça / Beato / Alcântara / Caparica (rotating). Five-month open-air season (13 May–18 Oct 2026, 120+ films, ~half premieres) alternating between the cloister of Igreja da Graça (Largo da Graça 94) and the terrace of Palácio do Grilo (Calçada do Duque de Lafões 1, Beato); live music at sunset before the film, doors ~19:00; street food (Secret Oven, Lisbon Kebab Station). Also runs beach screenings at Praia de São João da Caparica (with Bicho d'Água). Tickets ~€15. https://www.theblackcatcinema.com — Instagram @theblackcatcinema
- **Cinecapitólio Rooftop** — Parque Mayer (off Avenida da Liberdade). Rooftop open-air cinema atop the Capitólio; classics and recent hits with HD sound via headphones, loungers and blankets; 2026 season 27 May–26 Sept, Wed–Sun ~21:30 (21:00 in Sept), tickets ~€14; 2026 director cycles (Lynch, Nolan, Burton, Scorsese) plus a "Rostos do Variedades" cycle with the Cinemateca and São Jorge. https://capitolio.pt
- **CineConchas** — Quinta das Conchas, Lumiar / Alta de Lisboa. Free family-friendly open-air screenings in the garden (2026: ~25 June–11 July, Thu–Sat ~21:45), national and international titles; run by Centro Social da Musgueira within Festas de Lisboa/EGEAC. https://www.egeac.pt
- **Cinema no Verão (Jardim do Torel)** — Torel / Anjos. Free August open-air cinema in the Torel garden, Fridays and Saturdays ~21:00, iconic films across genres and decades; an EGEAC/Festas de Lisboa programme; listings via AgendaLX. https://www.agendalx.pt
- **Cinema ao ar livre no Parque Mayer (Festas de Lisboa)** — Parque Mayer. Free EGEAC open-air sessions on Fridays/Saturdays in August at Parque Mayer, separate from the paid Cinecapitólio rooftop above it. https://www.festasdelisboa.com
- **Cinema no Largo do Intendente (one-off live-score nights)** — Intendente. Festas de Lisboa special open-air sessions, e.g. Buster Keaton's "The General" with live score (18 June 2026, ~22:00, free). https://www.festasdelisboa.com — Instagram @festasdelisboa
- **Cine Pop / sessions at Cinema Roma** — Areeiro. Cinephile collective programming classic films; the restored 1957 Cinema Roma is its main home, with occasional open-air-style sessions. Instagram @cinepoplx
- **Cinema ao Ar Livre & Cinema Drive-In** — greater Lisbon (mobile/drive-in operator). Pop-up open-air and drive-in screenings around the metro area. https://cinemaaoarlivre.pt
- **Upon Angels "Open-Air Cinema"** — Anjos (Upon Lisbon Prime Residences). Hotel-rooftop open-air cinema tied to the terrace's seasonal programming. https://www.stayupon.com
- **CCB / MAAT / Belém riverfront summer screenings** — Belém. Institutions occasionally run free outdoor film nights on the riverside lawns in summer; check seasonally. https://ccb.pt , https://maat.pt
- **Festas de Lisboa open-air cinema circuit** — citywide (June–August). Umbrella festival behind several free garden/miradouro screenings each summer; programme published annually by EGEAC. https://www.festasdelisboa.com
- **Cine Bela Vista** — Sete Rios. Long-running vintage cinema (since 1943) screening Portuguese classics and international favourites with seasonal open-air-style sessions. **[MAY HAVE CLOSED — small, very old venue; current operating status unconfirmed]**
- *Aggregator note:* Time Out Lisboa, Lisboa Secreta, NiT and idealista publish the definitive annual "cinema ao ar livre" round-ups each May/June — best single sources for that summer's full venue list. https://www.timeout.pt/lisboa/pt/filmes/chegou-o-verao-e-ha-cinema-ao-ar-livre-em-lisboa , https://lisboasecreta.co/cinemas-ar-livre-em-lisboa/

## 7. Arthouse & Independent Cinemas + Film Festivals

### Cinemas
- **Cinemateca Portuguesa – Museu do Cinema** — Avenida da Liberdade / Príncipe Real (Rua Barata Salgueiro 39). National film archive, museum and repertory cinema; classics, restorations, retrospectives, the 39 Degraus café; also the Bairro Azul archive (ANIM) and **Cinemateca Júnior** sessions for kids. https://www.cinemateca.pt
- **Cinema São Jorge** — Avenida da Liberdade. EGEAC-run grand 1950 cinema; the festival hub of the city (IndieLisboa, DocLisboa, MOTELX, Queer Lisboa, MONSTRA, Olhares do Mediterrâneo, Festa do Cinema Italiano/Francês all use it). https://cinemasaojorge.pt — Instagram @cinemasaojorge
- **Cinema Ideal** — Chiado / Bairro Alto (Rua do Loreto 15). Oldest cinema site in Portugal (Salão Ideal, 1904; reopened 2014), run by Midas Filmes; arthouse and auteur programming. https://www.cinemaideal.pt — Instagram @cinemaidealLISBOA
- **Cinema Medeia Nimas (Espaço Nimas)** — Avenidas Novas (Avenida 5 de Outubro 42-B). Medeia Filmes arthouse cinema; 2026 retrospectives announced for Eustache/Garrel, then full Agnès Varda, Haneke and Otar Iosseliani, plus ≥6 Portuguese premieres. https://medeiafilmes.com/cinemas/cinema-medeia-nimas
- **Cinema Medeia Monumental** — Saldanha (Praça Duque de Saldanha). Medeia's multi-screen arthouse cinema, independent and auteur films. https://medeiafilmes.com
- **Cinema Roma** — Areeiro (Avenida de Roma). Restored 1957 cinema hosting Cine Pop classic-film cycles and special screenings. Instagram @cinepoplx
- **Cinema Fernando Lopes** — Marquês de Pombal / Campolide (Universidade Lusófona / ULHT campus). University screening room used as an IndieLisboa venue and for cinephile sessions. https://indielisboa.com
- **Culturgest (auditoriums)** — Avenidas Novas (Rua Arco do Cego, CGD building). Hosts festival screenings (IndieLisboa, DocLisboa) and its own film cycles. https://www.culturgest.pt
- **Brotéria (cinema/cycles)** — Bairro Alto / Príncipe Real (Rua de São Pedro de Alcântara 3). Jesuit cultural centre with an auditorium running occasional film cycles, talks and exhibitions. https://broteria.org — Instagram @broteria_cultura
- **UCI Cinemas – El Corte Inglés** — São Sebastião. Multiplex used as an overflow festival venue (Festa do Cinema Italiano and others). https://www.ucicinemas.pt
- **Cinema City Alvalade / Cinema City Campo Pequeno** — Alvalade / Campo Pequeno. Commercial chain that occasionally carries indie and VO titles. https://www.cinemacity.pt
- **NOS Cinemas (Amoreiras, Colombo, Vasco da Gama, Alvaláxia)** — various. Mainstream chain useful for VO releases and festival overflow. https://www.cinemas.nos.pt
- *Closed / historical (NOT active venues, listed to avoid confusion):* Eden, King, Quarteto, Londres, Condes, Império, Tivoli cinema, Alvalade cinema, Cine Paraíso. **[CLOSED]**

### Film festivals
- **IndieLisboa – International Independent Film Festival** — citywide (São Jorge, Culturgest, Cinemateca, Ideal, Fernando Lopes). Major indie/auteur/experimental festival; 2026 (23rd) edition 30 April–10 May, 241 films. https://indielisboa.com — Instagram @indielisboa
- **DocLisboa** — São Jorge, Culturgest, Cinemateca, Ideal. Portugal's leading documentary festival (since 2007); 2026 edition ~mid–late October. https://doclisboa.org — Instagram @doclisboa
- **MOTELX – Lisbon International Horror Film Festival** — Cinema São Jorge. Horror and fantastic cinema; 20th edition Sept 2026 with a Warm-Up week. https://www.motelx.org — Instagram @motelx
- **LEFFEST – Lisbon & Sintra Film Festival** — Lisbon and Sintra (Medeia Nimas and others). Paulo Branco's auteur festival; November 2026 edition. https://leffest.com — Instagram @leffest_
- **Queer Lisboa – International Queer Film Festival** — Cinema São Jorge / Cinemateca Portuguesa. Long-running LGBTQ+ film festival; 30th edition 18–26 September 2026. https://queerlisboa.pt — Instagram @queerlisboa
- **MONSTRA – Festival de Animação de Lisboa** — São Jorge and partner venues. Lisbon's animation festival; 25th edition 12–22 March 2026 (theme Nature & Sustainability, 490+ films). https://www.monstrafestival.com — Instagram @monstrafestival
- **Festa do Cinema Italiano** — São Jorge, UCI El Corte Inglés, Cinemateca. Italian film festival; 2026 edition 9–17 April (Claudia Cardinale retrospective; opening La grazia by Sorrentino). https://festadocinemaitaliano.com
- **Festa do Cinema Francês** — São Jorge + Institut Français de Lisbonne. French film festival, every October. https://festadocinemafrances.com — Instagram @institutfrancaisportugal
- **Festa do Cinema Alemão (German Film Festival)** — Lisbon (São Jorge / Goethe-Institut). Annual German-cinema showcase run with the Goethe-Institut. https://www.goethe.de/ins/pt/pt/index.html
- **Olhares do Mediterrâneo – Cinema no Feminino** — Cinema São Jorge. Portugal's women's-cinema festival focused on Mediterranean film; 2026 edition late September (~27–30 Sept). https://www.olharesdomediterraneo.org — Instagram @olhares.do.mediterraneo
- **MOSTRA Espanha** — Lisbon (multiple venues incl. Instituto Cervantes/São Jorge). Spanish-language cultural festival with a strong cinema strand. https://www.mostraespanha.org
- **Caminhos do Cinema Português** — Coimbra-based but tours; Lisbon screenings via São Jorge/Cinemateca. https://caminhos.info
- **Amadora BD film strand / animation cycles** — see MONSTRA and Amadora BD (category 44) for animation crossover.
- *Listings & platforms:* Cinecartaz (Público), Cartaz Cultural Lisboa and Clube de Cinema list every screening citywide. https://cinecartaz.publico.pt , https://cartazculturallisboa.pt/cinema-em-lisboa/ , https://clubedecinema.pt

## 31. Immersive & Experiential

### Immersive exhibitions & light/projection shows
- **Immersivus Gallery Lisboa** — Amoreiras (Reservatório da Mãe d'Água das Amoreiras). Flagship 360º video-mapping shows by Ocubo — "Impressive Monet & Brilliant Klimt", "Living Van Gogh", "Michelangelo & Da Vinci", "Mysterious Egypt" rotations; Tue–Sun. https://www.lisboa.immersivus.com — also https://feverup.com/m/93441/en
- **Dalí: A Experiência Imersiva** — Lisbon (central). Immersive Dalí show with interactive installations and a VR component. https://immersivedaliportugal.com
- **Dalí Cybernetics** — Lisbon. Separate Dalí immersive/AI-and-projection experience (distinct from the show above). https://feverup.com/m/141292/en
- **Frida Kahlo – A Vida de um Ícone** — Lisbon. Immersive exhibition on the life of Frida Kahlo. https://feverup.com/m/118237
- **"Ah, Amália" – Immersive Fado Experience** — Marvila (8 Marvila, Praça David Leandro da Silva 15-16). Eight interactive rooms (VR, hologram, video-mapping, 360º audiovisual) tributing Amália Rodrigues; daily ~11:00–19:00. https://ah-amalia.pt
- **Lisbon Legends – The Immersive Experience** — Chiado (Convento do Carmo ruins). 360º multimedia show on the legends of Lisbon's seven hills, soundtrack by Noiserv, ~41 min, nightly (PT/EN); tickets via Fever. https://www.lisbonlegends.com
- **Quake – Lisbon Earthquake Museum (Museu do Terramoto)** — Belém (near Avenida da Índia). ~90-min immersive route on the 1755 earthquake with simulators, video-mapping, smells and heat effects. https://lisbonquake.com
- **Lisboa Story Centre** — Baixa (Praça do Comércio / Terreiro do Paço). ~60-min multimedia journey through Lisbon's history including a shaking-floor 1755 earthquake re-enactment. https://www.lisboastorycentre.pt
- **Khufu's Horizon: Journey to Ancient Egypt** — Terreiro do Paço (metro station space). Immersive Ancient Egypt exhibition; tickets via Fever. https://feverup.com
- **Machu Picchu & the Golden Empires** — Terreiro do Paço (metro station space). Immersive lost-city exhibition; tickets via Fever. https://feverup.com
- **Museum of Illusions Lisbon** — Baixa/Chiado area. Optical-illusion rooms and interactive exhibits. https://museumofillusions.pt
- **Fado in Chiado** — Chiado (Cine-Teatro Gymnásio, Rua da Misericórdia 14). Daily ~50-min staged fado show with projected Lisbon imagery — a light immersive/experiential format. https://fadoinchiado.com
- **Immersive shows on Fever (multi-pass)** — Amoreiras / various. Fever bundles Immersivus and other light-and-sound experiences; best place to track new pop-ups. https://feverup.com/en/lisbon/immersive-experiences

- **Puzzle Room Lisbon** — Saldanha (Rua São Sebastião da Pedreira 100A). Three classic escape rooms (up to 10 players, 60 min). https://puzzleroom.pt

- **Escape Hunt Lisboa** — Lisbon (central). International escape-room brand with themed live games. https://escapehunt.com/pt/lisbon/en/
- **Safarka Escape Room** — Campo de Ourique. Escape game featuring live professional actors throughout the story. **[MAY HAVE CLOSED — listings inconsistent; confirm current status]**

### VR / free-roam arenas
- **Another World – VR Arena** — Marvila (Avenida Infante D. Henrique). Billed as Europe's largest free-roam VR arena (~700 m², up to ~20 players); VR escape rooms, films and games; opened 2024. https://vr-arena.pt — Instagram @anotherworld.vr
- **Vive Virtual** — Parque das Nações (rooftop of Decathlon). ~500 m² VR complex with simulators, multiplayer arenas and 100+ immersive games, open daily. https://feverup.com/m/78790
- **Centro de Realidade Virtual (VR Center)** — Parque das Nações. Multiplayer VR games, immersive escape rooms, sports and educational experiences. https://www.timeout.pt/lisboa/pt/coisas-para-fazer/centro-de-realidade-virtual
- **EscapeVR** — mobile / various (Portugal-wide). 40+ VR experiences including VR escape rooms, action and corporate events; mobile pop-ups. https://escapevr.pt

### Planetarium & science
- **Planetário Calouste Gulbenkian (Planetário de Marinha)** — Belém. Navy-run planetarium with a hybrid Zeiss fulldome digital + optical system; astronomy shows plus music/science fulldome sessions, English sessions and school programme (2026 season ~Jan–June). https://cultura.marinha.pt/pt/planetario
- **Pavilhão do Conhecimento – Ciência Viva** — Parque das Nações. Interactive hands-on science centre with immersive exhibits and dome/AV experiences (overlaps with kids/science category). https://www.pavconhecimento.pt
- *Watch list:* Lisbon's immersive-attraction scene turns over fast — new video-mapping and "experience" pop-ups appear via Fever and Visit Lisboa each season; check both for newcomers. https://www.visitlisboa.com/en/events

## 44. Photography & Illustration

### Photography — festivals & fairs
- **Imago Lisboa – Festival Fotográfico de Lisboa** — citywide (~10 cultural spaces incl. SNBA, MNAC). Photography festival across Sept–Nov; recent edition themed on racism/xenophobia ("Breaking the Silence – Walking Together"). https://imagolisboa.pt — Instagram @imagolisboa — also via MNAC http://museuartecontemporanea.gov.pt
- **Lisbon Street Photo Fest** — Belém (Faculdade de Belas-Artes / FBAUL). International street-photography festival; 2nd edition 25–27 September 2026 — exhibitions, talks, workshops, photowalks, portfolio reviews, prizes, Fujifilm stations. https://www.lisbonstreet.photo — Instagram @lisbonstreet.photo
- **Lisbon Photobook Fair (Feira do Livro de Fotografia de Lisboa)** — Arquivo Municipal de Lisboa – Fotográfico (Rua da Palma 246, Intendente/Mouraria). Annual photobook & photozine fair, last weekend of November. https://www.lisbonphotobookfair.com — Instagram @lisbonphotobookfair
- *Photography venues to mine for shows:* Gulbenkian/CAM, MNAC-Chiado, MAAT, Culturgest and Museu de Lisboa all run major photography exhibitions; Time Out keeps a running "exposições de fotografia" list. https://www.timeout.pt/lisboa/pt/arte/exposicoes-de-fotografia-para-ver-em-lisboa

### Photography — galleries & spaces
- **KGaleria (KameraPhoto)** — Lisbon (central). Gallery of the KameraPhoto documentary-photography collective; rotating shows of Portuguese and foreign photographers, specialist publications and fine prints. https://lisboa.convida.pt/en/poi/see-do/kgaleria-kameraphoto-3894 — Instagram @kameraphoto
- **Hangar – Centro de Investigação Artística** — Graça (Rua Damasceno Monteiro 12). Artist-run research centre and residency with 4–6 exhibitions a year, strong on photography, plus talks, seminars and video screenings. https://hangar.com.pt — Instagram @hangar.cia
- **Galeria Foco** — Lisbon. Contemporary, interdisciplinary gallery (founded by architect Benjamin Gonthier) showing Portuguese and international artists, including photography. https://galeriafoco.com — Instagram @galeria_foco
- **Procur.arte (Procurarte)** — Lisbon (cultural association/gallery). Photography and contemporary-art exhibitions (e.g. Augusto Brázio's "O Bairro"), residencies and projects. https://www.procurarte.org
- **Underdogs Gallery** — Marvila. Urban-culture gallery with photography in its mix, a public-art programme, art store and the experimental "Capsule" space. https://www.under-dogs.net — Instagram @underdogs_gallery
- **Galerias Salgadeiras** — Bairro Alto (and Av. dos EUA space). Contemporary gallery (since 2003) showing painting and photography. https://salgadeiras.com
- **MACAM (Museu de Arte Contemporânea Armando Martins)** — Alcântara. New private museum showing photography (Cindy Sherman, Helena Almeida) from the Américo/Armando collection. https://www.macam.pt
- **Atelier-Museu Júlio Pomar** — Madragoa / Santos (Rua do Vale). Museum with temporary exhibitions, talks and occasional photography crossovers. https://www.ateliermuseujuliopomar.pt
- **Brotéria** — Bairro Alto (Rua de São Pedro de Alcântara 3). Cultural centre that also programmes photography/visual-arts exhibitions. https://broteria.org

### Photowalks & photography communities
- **Lisboa Photo Community (Meetup)** — citywide. Informal all-levels group running social photowalks and meetups. https://www.meetup.com/lisbon-photo-community-group
- **Lisboa Photography Club** — citywide. Instagram-based club organising walks and meetups. Instagram @lisboa_photography_club
- **Photowalks.pt** — citywide. Guided Lisbon photography tours/photowalks operator. https://photowalks.pt

- **Fascínio da Fotografia** — Lisbon (blog/agenda). Long-running Portuguese photography agenda listing exhibitions citywide — strong discovery source. https://fasciniodafotografia.com
