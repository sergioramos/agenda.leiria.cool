# Batch 06 — Ceramics, creative workshops, creative hubs, DIY spaces, makerspaces & volunteering

Scope note: this batch covers categories 4, 5, 8, 9, 38 and 46. Entries are deduplicated within the batch; a few venues span multiple categories (e.g. Studio Marvila, Fábrica Moderna, FabLab Lisboa, Repair Café) and are placed under their primary category with a cross-reference. Information current as of June 2026; seasonal and closure flags noted inline.

## 4. Ceramics & pottery

**Wheel-throwing & hand-building studios**

- **Disco Wheel** — Cais do Sodré. Wheel-throwing studio (8 wheels) plus shop selling Portuguese/international ceramics; drop-in workshops (~€60 incl. glaze + firing), private group rentals; opened May 2022. discowheel.com / [@discowheel](https://instagram.com/discowheel)
- **hALL (hAll Atelier)** — Campolide (near Príncipe Real). Hand-building (~€65) and pottery-wheel (~€80) classes plus "hugging sessions"; doubles as coworking/event space; opened 2020 by Diana Marques. hallatelier.com / [@hall.atelier](https://instagram.com/hall.atelier)
- **Studio Marvila** — Marvila (Rua Capitão Leitão 64). Pottery-wheel and hand-building classes (~€55), "Sip & Spin" wheel + natural-wine sessions (~€75); small evening/weekend classes (cross-listed cat. 5 natural wine). studiomarvila.com / [@studiomarvila](https://instagram.com/studiomarvila)
- **PURACERÂMICA** — Alcântara. Lisbon's original pottery-painting studio (since 2022): paint-your-own (100+ bisque pieces), plus hand-building and wheel workshops; walk-ins subject to availability. puraceramica.pt / [@puraceramica](https://instagram.com/puraceramica)
- **Klæy Kreative** — Areeiro/Avenidas Novas (Av. Frei Miguel Contreiras 54D, near Roma-Areeiro). Paint-your-own pottery studio, pre-made bisque pieces from ~€19.95; sessions to 2.5h, up to 20 people; closed Wednesdays. klaeykreative.com
- **TOCA** — Arroios. Ceramics atelier + shop (opened May 2025, by Isabela Schöffler); workshops from ~€20 that mix clay with Pilates, cake decorating, painting or wine. [@toca.ceramica](https://instagram.com/toca.ceramica)
- **Tijolo — Cerâmica e Joalharia** — Lisbon (central). Combined ceramics + jewellery studio/coworking + shop (since 2021, Ana Maria Lopes); beginner ceramics classes through the week, kiln access, independent-maker workspace (cross-listed cat. 5 jewellery). tijolo.pt
- **Cerâmica Bairro Alto** — Bairro Alto. Atelier-gallery-school by Maria João Ribeiro; ~1h create-your-own sessions and technique workshops (manual + wheel intro), max 6 per group. ceramicabairroalto.com
- **Cécile Mestelan / Olho Cerâmica** — Chiado/Cais do Sodré. Wheel and hand-building classes/workshops ~3x weekly (founded 2013 by Cécile Mestelan); on the Rede de Artes e Ofícios. cecilemestelan.com / redearteseoficios.pt
- **Olaria Lisboa** — Lisbon. Teaching studio run by Inês Diana Salgado (30+ yrs); pottery, modelling, sculpture and tile painting in PT/EN/ES. olarialisboa.com
- **Eva Lé Ceramics** — Grande Lisboa. Group/private wheel classes, hand-building, kids' workshops; basement coworking "Cerâmica Club" with wheel, benches and glazing room. evaleceramics.com
- **Estúdio Torto** — Beato. Free-form sessions and technique classes (plaster moulds, slip-casting); space for resident ceramists and beginners (Alexandre da Silva & Vincent Richeux).
- **Frolamics Studio** — Estrela/Lapa/Santos. Quick sessions and longer construction/glazing courses; experimental glazes, founded by Nadia Frolova. [@frolamics](https://instagram.com/frolamics)
- **Joana Passos Studio** — Santa Maria Maior (Baixa Pombalina). Private hand-throwing lessons; also hosts resident ceramists.
- **Caulino Ceramics** — central Lisbon (Rua de S. Mamede ao Caldas 28). Studio/shop (founded 2002, Cátia Pessoa); flexible 2-hour weekday workshops, summer courses, kids' parties. caulinoceramics.com
- **Cerâmica S. Vicente (Atelier)** — São Vicente (Graça/Alfama side). Family studio (~12 yrs) doing fully manual traditional tile-to-glaze work; workshops in traditional Portuguese ceramics. ceramica-svicentelisboa.com
