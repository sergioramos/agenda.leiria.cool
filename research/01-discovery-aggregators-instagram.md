## 1. Aggregators & listing sites

Free-Sunday / free-admission museum programmes
- **State museums/monuments (DGPC) — free Sundays & holidays for Portugal residents** — Citywide — Present Portuguese ID; each Sunday counts as 1 of ~52 free annual days. Covers MNAA, Museu Nacional do Azulejo, Museu do Chiado/MNAC, Mosteiro dos Jerónimos, Torre de Belém, etc. (Note: free entry to residents is generally year-round on Sundays, not only first Sunday.)
- **Museu Calouste Gulbenkian** — Avenidas Novas — Main museum **closed for renovation until July 2026**; CAM (Centro de Arte Moderna) is open with free admission every Sunday 14:00–18:00. gulbenkian.pt — *(Seasonal/temporary closure)*
- **MNAA (Museu Nacional de Arte Antiga)** — Alcântara/Madragoa — **[MAY HAVE CLOSED — temporary]** Reported closed for renovations as of May 2026; check before visiting. museudearteantiga.pt
- **Museu Nacional do Azulejo** — Beato/Xabregas — Free 1st Sunday of the month (and Sundays for residents). museudoazulejo.gov.pt
- **MAAT** — Belém — Free first Sunday of the month, 10:00–13:00 (residents). maat.pt
- **Museu de Lisboa (Palácio Pimenta / Teatro Romano / Santo António)** — Various — Free Sunday until 14:00 for Lisbon residents. museudelisboa.pt
- **Museu da Água (EPAL)** — Free first Sunday of month. epal.pt
- **52-days residents programme** — Portuguese residents: free entry to national museums/monuments/palaces on ~52 days/year with ID.

Free concert / open-air series (seasonal)
- **Jardim de Verão (Gulbenkian)** — Avenidas Novas — Free summer festival: concerts, DJ sets, films, talks; tickets collected same day. gulbenkian.pt/jardim-de-verao — *(Seasonal — summer)*
- **Millennium Festival ao Largo** — Largo de São Carlos, Chiado — Free open-air opera, classical, dance and ballet; ~July. festivalaolargo.pt — *(Seasonal — July)*
- **Festas de Lisboa / Santos Populares arraiais** — Alfama, Graça, Mouraria, Bica, Madragoa, Castelo, etc. — Free June street festivals, Marchas Populares (12 June, Av. da Liberdade) and Santo António concerts (programmed by EGEAC). egeac.pt — *(Seasonal — June)*
- **Free open-air concerts (summer)** — Jardim da Estrela, Parque Eduardo VII and other gardens — Free weekend programming in summer. *(Seasonal — summer)*
- **Free viewpoints (miradouros)** — Graça, Senhora do Monte, São Pedro de Alcântara, Portas do Sol — Free, with frequent kiosk DJ/sunset programming in summer.
