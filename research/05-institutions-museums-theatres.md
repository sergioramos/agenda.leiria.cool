# Batch 05 — Institutions, museums, theatres, foreign institutes, universities, kids & circus

## 11. Major institutions & theatres

- **Fundação Calouste Gulbenkian** — Avenidas Novas (Praça de Espanha / São Sebastião). Vast cultural campus: classical concerts (Gulbenkian Orquestra & Coro), jazz, contemporary music, exhibitions, talks, and the free summer festival **Jardim de Verão** (concerts, DJ sets, films, family activities across several weekends, seasonal). New Gulbenkian Música director Fredrik Andersson opens the 2026/27 season (120+ performances). "Rising Stars – Portas Abertas" free concerts (spring); a "Silent Party" for Noite dos Museus (17 May 2026). Website: https://gulbenkian.pt/ · Música agenda: https://gulbenkian.pt/musica/agenda/ · Instagram: @fcgulbenkian
- **CAM – Centro de Arte Moderna Gulbenkian** — Avenidas Novas (inside the Gulbenkian gardens). Reopened 2024 after redevelopment by Kengo Kuma; modern/contemporary art exhibitions plus performance, talks and late-opening events. Agenda: https://gulbenkian.pt/cam/agenda/ · Instagram: @camgulbenkian
- **Museu Calouste Gulbenkian – Coleção do Fundador** — Avenidas Novas. The founder's collection wing; **[PARTIALLY CLOSED]** for renovation until around mid-2026, but broader Foundation programming continues. Agenda: https://gulbenkian.pt/museu/agenda/
- **CCB – Centro Cultural de Belém** — Belém. Large multi-hall complex: opera, symphonic and chamber concerts (Grande Auditório), dance, theatre, fado cycle, festivals (hosts LEFFEST in November) and the summer "CCB de Verão" live-concert series (seasonal). Website: https://www.ccb.pt/ · Tickets: https://ccb.bol.pt/ · Instagram: @ccb_pt
- **MAC/CCB – Museu de Arte Contemporânea (ex-Coleção Berardo)** — Belém. Contemporary art museum within CCB showing the Berardo, Ellipse and Teixeira de Freitas collections; "Música no Museu" Saturday concert series, guided tours (incl. English), conferences, educational activities; Noite dos Museus. Website: https://www.ccb.pt/en/macccb/
- **Garagem Sul (CCB)** — Belém. CCB's architecture and design exhibition space, with talks and architecture programming. Within https://www.ccb.pt/
- **Culturgest (Fundação CGD)** — Avenidas Novas (Campo Pequeno / Edifício Sede da CGD). Theatre, dance, a multi-concert music season (2026 names include Diamanda Galás, Oneohtrix Point Never, Tortoise, A Winged Victory for the Sullen), visual-arts exhibitions, talks; hosts IndieLisboa and IndieJúnior family sessions. Website: https://www.culturgest.pt/ · Instagram: @culturgest
- **MAAT – Museu de Arte, Arquitetura e Tecnologia** — Belém (riverside, EDP Foundation). Art/architecture/technology exhibitions, late openings, riverside programming and a 10th-anniversary year in 2026 (free-entry days). Participates in Noite dos Museus. Website: https://maat.pt/ · Agenda: https://maat.pt/en/agenda · 2026 exhibitions: https://www.maat.pt/en/programme/2026-exhibitions · Instagram: @maat_museum
- **Teatro Nacional D. Maria II** — Rossio / Baixa. National drama theatre. **[VENUE UNDER RENOVATION]** — reopens at Rossio 18 Sept 2026 (€9.8M PRR rehabilitation); until then programmes in other Lisbon and national spaces, plus the "Prólogo" pre-opening events (22 Jun–25 Jul 2026) and the "Cenários" thinking event (25–26 Jun). Website: https://www.tndm.pt/ · Instagram: @teatronacionaldmariaii
- **São Luiz Teatro Municipal** — Chiado (Rua António Maria Cardoso). EGEAC-run municipal theatre: theatre, ~36 concerts, dance, circus, opera, the FIMFA puppet festival, literature and film cycles; hosts kids/family shows. Website: https://www.teatrosaoluiz.pt/ · Instagram: @teatrosaoluiz
- **Teatro Nacional de São Carlos** — Chiado (Largo de São Carlos). Portugal's national opera house: opera, symphonic concerts, chamber music and ballet (Wagner, Tchaikovsky, Mahler, Mozart in 2025/26; Puccini's Suor Angelica/Gianni Schicchi June 2026). Website: https://www.saocarlos.pt/ · https://www.tnsc.pt/
- **Teatro da Trindade INATEL** — Chiado (Rua Nova da Trindade). Performing-arts theatre with two halls (Carmen Dolores, Estúdio); classic and contemporary drama, several premieres in the 2025/26 season. Website: https://teatrotrindade.inatel.pt/
- **Teatro Aberto** — Campolide (Praça de Espanha). Independent repertory theatre marking its 50th anniversary; drama and opera ("Por Todos Nós"); expanding with a studio room and covered terrace. Website: https://teatroaberto.com/
- **Teatro Tivoli BBVA** — Avenida da Liberdade. 1924 grand theatre for concerts (jazz, world, fado), comedy and touring shows (Wim Mertens, Avishai Cohen, GoGo Penguin, Chico César in 2026). Website: https://www.teatrotivolibbva.pt/ · Instagram: @teatrotivolibbva
- **LU.CA – Teatro Luís de Camões** — Belém (Calçada da Ajuda). EGEAC theatre dedicated to young audiences; 2026 theme "Voltar onde somos felizes" — theatre, dance, music, illustration for children, the "AH!" mezzanine exhibition for the youngest, and a FIMFA strand. Website: https://lucateatroluisdecamoes.pt/ · Instagram: @luca_teatro
- **Fundação Champalimaud (Centre for the Unknown)** — Belém (Algés/Pedrouços riverfront). Biomedical research centre with an auditorium hosting public talks, conferences and symposia (e.g. Champalimaud Research Symposium in autumn). Events: https://fchampalimaud.org/events · Instagram: @champalimaudfoundation
- **Teatro do Bairro** — Bairro Alto (Rua Luz Soriano). Independent theatre, cinema, dance and music venue, home to Companhia Teatro do Bairro and the Ar de Filmes cinema strand; intimate alternative programming. Website: https://teatrodobairro.org/
- **Teatro do Bairro Alto (TBA)** — Bairro Alto (Rua Tenente Raul Cascais). Municipal theatre for experimental, emerging and international performance; a seven-year programming cycle closes at the end of 2026. Website: https://teatrodobairroalto.pt/ · Instagram: @teatrodobairroalto
- **Cordoaria Nacional** — Belém/Junqueira. Vast former navy rope factory, now a venue for large contemporary-art exhibitions and cultural/immersive events. Tickets via partners: https://feverup.com/en/lisbon/venue/cordoaria-nacional

## 43. Circus & physical theatre

- **Chapitô** — Santa Catarina / Costa do Castelo (between Bairro Alto and Alfama, by the castle). Circus organisation founded 1981 (Teresa Ricou): professional circus school (Escola de Artes Circenses — Artes do Circo & Ofícios Circenses, plus evening courses), Companhia do Chapitô theatre company, performance venue, restaurant-bar "Chapitô à Mesa", famous terrace; monthly agenda and the seasonal "Circo no Castelo" (Saturdays in July, at Castelo de São Jorge). Website: https://chapito.org/ · Instagram: @o_chapito
- **Escola de Circo do Chapitô** — Costa do Castelo. The school strand: full-time professional training and children's/end-of-term exercise-shows. Facebook: escolachapito
