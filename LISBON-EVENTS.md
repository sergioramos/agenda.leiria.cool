# Lisbon Events — Master Directory

The most complete directory of places and sources for events in Lisbon, Portugal, compiled June 2026. Organised under the 49 brief categories. Each entry gives: name, neighbourhood/area, one line on what it hosts or announces, and a website and/or Instagram @handle.

**Deduplication:** venues that fit several categories get one full entry under their primary category and a short cross-reference (*see §N*) elsewhere. **Flags:** items that may have closed, are at risk, have relocated, or are temporarily shut for renovation are marked inline with **[FLAG]** and consolidated below. **Seasonal** items note their window.
